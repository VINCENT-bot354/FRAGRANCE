How to Order

Simple Steps to Get Your Fashion Items

Ordering Process

At CHARA FASHION AND FRAGRANCE, we've made ordering as simple as possible. Follow these easy steps to get your favorite fashion items delivered to you.

1

Browse Our Collection

Start by exploring our collection of clothes, shoes, and accessories. Use the filter options to narrow down your search by product type, category, or other specifications.

Click on any product to view detailed information, including available sizes, colors, and multiple product images from different angles.

2

Choose Your Products

Once you've found items you love, note down the product names, sizes, colors, and any other relevant details. You can view all available options on each product's detail page.

Take your time to review all product images and information to ensure you're selecting exactly what you want.

3

Contact Us to Order

When you're ready to place an order, you can contact us through one of the following methods:

  • WhatsApp: Send a message to {{ contact_info.whatsapp }}
  • Phone Call: Call us directly at {{ contact_info.phone }}
  • Instagram: Send a direct message to @{{ contact_info.instagram }}

You can also click the "Order Now" button on any product page, which will redirect you to your preferred contact method with a pre-filled message about the product.

4

Confirm Order Details

Once you contact us, we'll confirm the availability of your selected items, provide you with the total cost including any applicable delivery fees, and answer any questions you might have.

We'll also discuss delivery options and timeframes to ensure your order reaches you as conveniently as possible.

5

Payment and Delivery

We accept various payment methods including mobile money transfers and cash on delivery, depending on your location and preference.

After your payment is confirmed, we'll prepare your order for delivery or pickup, whichever you prefer. We'll provide tracking information where available and keep you updated on your order status.
